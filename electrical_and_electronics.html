Electrical and Electronics

Project Overview

Keysight Technologies is a leading provider of electronic design and test solutions for industries including telecommunications, networking, aerospace, and electronics. I worked as a Technical Writer, responsible for producing comprehensive hardware documentation for Keysight's electronic test and measurement instruments.

Problem Space & Business Needs

  • Lack of detailed user guidance for complex hardware products like oscilloscopes and signal analyzers.
  • Needed standardized documentation to support customers in hardware setup, testing, and troubleshooting.
  • Required scalable documentation to align with rapid product releases and evolving hardware features.

My Role & Deliverables

  • Created user manuals, hardware installation guides, and operational reference documents.
  • Developed service manuals including calibration procedures, safety standards, and compliance information.
  • Collaborated with hardware engineers and SMEs to document system specifications, troubleshooting steps, and component diagrams.

Outcomes & Business Impact

  • Reduced customer support tickets by 35% through detailed troubleshooting documentation.
  • Accelerated customer onboarding and product setup time by 40% with well-structured manuals.
  • Ensured compliance with international safety and regulatory standards through precise documentation.
