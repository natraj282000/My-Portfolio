Banking and Finance

Project Overview

I partnered with Access Microfinance Holdings, a Germany-based investment banking and financial services firm, as a freelance technical writer. I focused on documenting secure financial APIs and DevOps CI/CD workflows that support their banking and financial products. The API documentation primarily supports internal operations, including environmental and social management systems.

Problem Space & Business Needs

  • Needed robust API documentation to enable third-party integrations with their banking platforms.
  • Internal developers and DevOps teams lacked comprehensive CI/CD pipeline documentation for environments like Azure and Kubernetes.
  • Required simplified, client-facing documentation derived from highly technical engineering outputs to support business scalability and efficiency.

My Role & Deliverables

  • Created and maintained API references, DevOps documentation, and CI/CD runbooks using Markdown, Confluence, and GitHub.
  • Authored detailed deployment guides for Azure-based infrastructure and Kubernetes clusters.
  • Translated engineering release notes and complex technical content into clear, client-facing documents.
  • Streamlined DevOps documentation workflows, reducing friction for developers and improving pipeline efficiency.

Outcomes & Business Impact

  • Reduced developer onboarding time by 40% with well-structured documentation.
  • Improved deployment accuracy and ensured security compliance through precise CI/CD process documentation.
  • Enabled self-service for internal development teams and external partners, reducing reliance on engineering for operational tasks.
