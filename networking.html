Networking

Project Overview

Cisco Systems is a global leader in networking hardware and telecommunications equipment. As a technical writer, I worked on documenting the installation and configuration process of Cisco enterprise routers, producing detailed installation manuals and internal SOPs used by field engineers and system integrators.

Problem Space & Business Needs

  • Field engineers lacked centralized, updated, and consistent router installation documentation.
  • Complex router setup procedures required clear step-by-step guidance with visual aids.
  • Needed multilingual-ready documentation for global rollout and support.

My Role & Deliverables

  • Created installation guides for Cisco routers, including cabling, mounting, and initial setup.
  • Developed CLI-based configuration walkthroughs and quick start references.
  • Included annotated hardware diagrams and port labeling instructions to assist in physical deployment.
  • Collaborated with network engineers and product managers to validate procedures in lab environments.

Outcomes & Business Impact

  • Improved field team efficiency by 30% due to streamlined documentation and visuals.
  • Reduced onboarding time for new support staff by providing modular documentation templates.
  • Increased customer satisfaction with reduced configuration errors and clearer instructions.
